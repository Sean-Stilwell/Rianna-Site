    About Me


    I'm a fourth-year student studying second language teaching at the University of Ottawa, specializing in teaching French as a second language. I am also minoring in translation from French into English. I am currently on the Dean's Honour List and have been the recipient of a variety of awards, from academic bursaries to the prestigious Governor General's Medal for Academic Excellence, and the Undergraduate Research Opportunity Program Scholarship.

    In addition to my studies, I teach beginner and intermediate level FLS conversation workshops at the University of Ottawa’s Official Languages and Bilingualism Institute, and work at the Canadian Parents for French national office as an administrator for the virtual tutoring program. I am also proud to be an ambassador for ACPI's #teacher5étoiles campaign.
